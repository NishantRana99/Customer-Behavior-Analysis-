# Customer Behavior Analysis Using Python, SQL & Power BI

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to understand purchasing patterns, customer preferences, and the factors influencing buying decisions. The objective is to generate actionable insights that help businesses improve customer engagement, optimize marketing strategies, and increase sales.

---

##  Business Problem

A leading retail company wants to gain a deeper understanding of its customers' shopping behavior. The company has observed changes in purchasing patterns across demographics, product categories, and sales channels.

**Business Question:**
How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?

---

##  Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer shopping information, including:

* Customer ID
* Age and Gender
* Product Category
* Purchase Amount
* Subscription Status
* Shipping Type
* Review Rating
* Previous Purchases
* Payment Method
* Discount Applied

---

##  Tools & Technologies Used

* **Python:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn
* **SQL:** Data querying and business analysis
* **Power BI:** Interactive dashboard creation
* **Jupyter Notebook:** Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

---

##  Project Workflow

### 1. Data Preparation (Python)

* Imported and explored the dataset
* Checked data types and missing values
* Cleaned and transformed the data
* Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### 2. Data Analysis (SQL)

Performed business analysis using SQL queries:

* Revenue analysis by gender
* Customer segmentation
* Subscription analysis
* Top-performing product categories
* Discount and spending analysis
* Repeat purchase analysis
* Customer loyalty insights

### 3. Dashboard Development (Power BI)

Developed an interactive dashboard containing:

* Total Customers
* Average Purchase Amount
* Average Review Rating
* Revenue by Category
* Sales by Category
* Revenue by Age Group
* Sales by Age Group
* Interactive filters for Gender, Subscription Status, Category, and Shipping Type

---

##  Key Insights

* Young Adult customers generated the highest revenue.
* Clothing and Accessories contributed the largest share of sales.
* Subscription status significantly influenced purchasing behavior.
* Customer spending patterns varied across different age groups and product categories.
* Review ratings helped identify highly preferred product categories.

---

##  Business Recommendations

* Implement targeted marketing campaigns for high-value customer segments.
* Introduce loyalty programs to increase repeat purchases.
* Focus promotional activities on top-performing product categories.
* Provide personalized offers based on customer demographics and purchasing history.
* Enhance customer experience by analyzing review ratings and feedback.
